Description (est)

Rutt Kresling kirjeldab oma rasket elusaatust, olles elanud läbi nii juuni- kui ka märtsiküüditamise, arreteerimise, isa kaotuse ja pika lahusoleku emast. Seda kõike aga ilma kibestumuse ja kaeblemiseta.

Description (eng)

Rutt Kresling speaks about her difficult life, having lived through two deportations, (in March and in June), being arrested, losing her father and being separated from her mother for a long time. In spite of this she recalls her story without complaining and being bitter.
