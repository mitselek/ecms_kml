Description (est)

Ain Haas kirjeldab oma vanemate põgenemislugu Rootsi ning edasist emigreerumist Ühendriikidessesse. Samuti on vaatluse all side Eestiga: rahvamuusika viljelemine, eestlus, eesti keel ning kontaktid Eesti sugulastega.

Description (eng)

Ain Haas speaks about his parent's escape to Sweden and further emigration to the USA. He also discusses his ties with Estonia, the blossoming of Estonian folk music, Estonian language, Estonianness and contact with his Estonian relatives.
