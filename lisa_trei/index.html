Description (est)

Lisa Trei kirjeldab oma vanavanemate siirdumist Ühendriikidessesse ja enda sidet Eestiga ning siinsete juurte otsimist. Samuti kirjeldab Lisa Trei oma ajakirjanikutööaastaid Eestis, mistõttu oli ta tunnistajaks mitmetele olulistele taasiseseisvumisperioodil aset leidnud sündmustele.

Description (eng)

Lisa Trei recalls the story of her grandparents’ escape to the US and describes her ties with Estonia and looking for her Estonian roots. She also reflects on the years she spent working as a journalist in Estonia and how she was a witness to many of the important events during the regaining of independence.
