Description (est)

Eha-Kay Uibo annab ülevaate oma pere küüditamisest 1941. aastal ning raskest elust Siberis, mida aitas trotsida usk. Samuti on kõne all Eestisse tagasipääsemine ning elu kodumaal.

Description (eng)

Eha-Kay Uibo speaks about the deportation of her parents in 1941, the difficulties of their life in Siberia and how their faith helped them endure it. She also discusses returning to Estonia and life in her homeland.
